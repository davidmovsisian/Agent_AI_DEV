## Research Papers

### Foundational Papers
1. **"Attention Is All You Need"** (2017) - Vaswani et al.
   - The transformer architecture that powers modern LLMs
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762

2. **"Language Models are Few-Shot Learners"** (2020) - Brown et al. (GPT-3)
   - Introduced few-shot learning capabilities of large models
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.14165

3. **"Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models"** (2022)
   - Wei et al. - Groundbreaking work on CoT reasoning
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903

### Agent-Specific Papers
4. **"ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models"** (2023)
   - Yao et al. - The ReAct pattern for agents
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629

5. **"Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks"** (2020)
   - Lewis et al. - Original RAG paper
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.11401

6. **"Tree of Thoughts: Deliberate Problem Solving with Large Language Models"** (2023)
   - Yao et al. - Advanced reasoning technique
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.10601

7. **"Reflexion: Language Agents with Verbal Reinforcement Learning"** (2023)
   - Shinn et al. - Self-reflection in agents
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.11366

8. **"HuggingGPT: Solving AI Tasks with ChatGPT and its Friends"** (2023)
   - Shen et al. - Multi-model agent orchestration
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.17580
